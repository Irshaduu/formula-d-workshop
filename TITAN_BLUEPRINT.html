Inventory Auto-Sync — Signal Engine (6 Handlers)
⚡ Workshop Consumption Signals (3 handlers)
1
New spare added to job card
→ Deduct full qty from warehouse stock
2
Quantity changed (same part)
→ pre_save snapshot → post_save delta → deduct only the difference
3
Part name changed
→ Restore old part stock fully → Deduct new part stock fully
4
Spare line deleted
→ Restore full qty back to warehouse stock
Add "Oil Filter" x2   →  Oil Filter: 10 → 8
Change qty to 5      →  Oil Filter: 8 → 5 (delta -3)
Change to "Air Filter" →  Oil Filter: 5 → 10 (restore)
                        →  Air Filter: 7 → 2 (deduct)
Delete spare line    →  Air Filter: 2 → 7 (restore)
🏭 Supplier Restock Signals (3 handlers)
5
New restock item created
→ Increase warehouse stock by full qty
6
Restock qty changed
→ pre_save snapshot + post_save delta → adjust stock by difference
7
Restock item/bill deleted
→ Reverse stock increase — fully restore to pre-bill state
📊 Stock Health System
Health = (current_stock / average_stock) × 100%
Green (50%+) Healthy — no action needed
Yellow (25–49%) Warning — reorder soon
Red (below 25%) Critical — order immediately
Create bill (5x Oil Filter) → Oil Filter: 10 → 15
Edit bill qty to 8         → Oil Filter: 15 → 18 (+3 delta)
Delete bill entirely      → Oil Filter: 18 → 10 (reverse -8)

Payment Systems — All Three Methods
💳 Single Job Payment
  • Update Bill Status — manually set amount received
  • Auto-discount: if received < bill → discount calculated
  • PENDING → PARTIAL → PAID
  • Methods: CASH UPI CARD TRANSFER
  • Invoice generated at any stage
SPARE PRICING (TWO-PRICE SYSTEM)
  • Shop Price = What you paid to parts shop (unit)
  • Customer Price = What customer pays (with markup)
  • Profit = Customer Price − (Shop Price × Qty)
👥 Bulk Payer — Fleet Cascade
  • Group multiple job cards under one customer/fleet
  • Pay lump-sum → Cascade Algorithm distributes oldest-first
  • JSON snapshot saved for precise reversal
  • Owner can reverse any bulk payment
  • Soft-deletable BulkPayer records (trash/restore)
Rs.10,000 lump sum → 5 jobs:
Job 1: Rs.3,000 → PAID  (7k left)
Job 2: Rs.5,000 → PAID  (2k left)
Job 3: Rs.2,000 → PAID  (0k left)
Job 4: Rs.0    → still pending
Job 5: Rs.0    → still pending
🏪 Spare Shop — Ledger Cascade
  • Full ledger per spare shop supplier
  • Total Purchases = Σ(unit_price × qty) for all linked items
  • Pay Individual Item OR lump-sum cascade (oldest-first)
  • JSON snapshot for precise reversal
  • Owner can reverse any shop payment
  • Unassigned Spares Hub: legacy stock not linked to jobs
  • Print ledger view available
vs SUPPLIES SHOPS
  • Spare Shops (Workshop) → buy FOR specific jobs
  • Supplies Shops (Inventory) → buy INTO warehouse stock

Performance Engineering — Built for 1M+ Records
⚡ Performance Guardrails
  • O(1) Data Retrieval — Server-side pagination enforced: 21 items/page (grids) · 50 items/page (floor lists)
  • N+1 Elimination — Strict select_related and prefetch_related across all list views
  • Denormalized Financials — JobCard.total_bill_amount is a physical DB column, not a runtime calc. Updated via update_totals() on each spare/labour save
  • B-Tree Indexes — db_index=True on: is_deleted, registration_number, admitted_date, delivered, updated_at
  • Composite Index — Dashboard pattern covered by [is_deleted, delivered, -updated_at]
  • Thread-Safe Bill Numbers — Auto JB-YY-NNN generation uses select_for_update() to prevent race conditions
  • AJAX Partials — Bulk payer, trash, delivered, supplier bills/payments — all AJAX pagination
